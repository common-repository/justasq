This plugin for WordPress helps users intergrate JustAsq into their WordPress powered site.

== Description ==

With JustAsq, you can provide personalised sales and customer support at scale.  
Deploy JustAsq on your website within minutes and start chatting with your visitors, even on the go.

For more information, visit [JustAsq](https://justasq.me).

== Installation ==

1. Search for 'JustAsq' from the 'Plugins' page and install
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

or

1. Upload `justasq.zip` to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where do I get the widget ID? =

You can get the ID for your widget from your account dashboard at [JustAsq](https://missioncontrol.justasq.me).

= Can I disable the widget? =

Yes, you can disable/enable the widget at will. You just have to check the box on the settings page for the plugin.

= Why does the widget show a blank page when I open i or not show at all? =

Most of the time, this is because you have not whitelisted your website URL when creating the widget.
Try whitelisting the proper domain and try again. If the problem persists, get in touch with our support team (support@justasq.me).
